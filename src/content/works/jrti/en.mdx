A [Kerbal Space Program](https://www.kerbalspaceprogram.com/) mod that streams your **HullCam VDS** camera feeds to a local webpage, so you can watch your rocket's onboard cameras from a browser while you play or broadcast them to OBS, your friends, or whatever else you can think of.

Spiritual successor to **OfCourseIStillLoveYou (OCISLY)** which was a mod similar to this one, but unfortunately not updated anymore. The goal is to push externalized camera viewing in KSP further, exploring how far the game can be taken with this kind of setup.
The main selling point was that this mod hooks into KSP's rendering pipeline to stream the camera feeds with support for all the coolest visual mods around the block !

Currently an experimental public release / proof of concept. It's versioned as 1.0.0 because the project is designed to stay iterative rather than wait for some imaginary "perfectly finished" state.
